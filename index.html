DRUMLINE BOOKING, ANSWERED

What is a drumline performance?

The Rhythm Messengers are a Chicago drumline: a crew of drummers and entertainers who deliver choreographed, high-energy percussion for live audiences. A performance can roam through a crowd, anchor a stage, or open your event with a burst of energy that gets everyone on their feet.

What events do the Rhythm Messengers perform at?

The Rhythm Messengers perform at corporate events, trade shows, festivals, sporting events, and private parties throughout Chicago and the surrounding area. Every set is tailored to the venue, from intimate ballrooms to festival main stages.

How do I hire a drumline in Chicago?

To book the Rhythm Messengers, send your event date, type, and location through the contact form below, or email rhythmmessengerschi@gmail.com. You'll get a fast reply with availability and a custom quote.

How much does it cost to hire a drumline?

Pricing for the Rhythm Messengers depends on your event type, location, performance length, and group size. Reach out with your details and we'll send a custom quote. There's no fixed ticket price.

How large is the group?

The Rhythm Messengers are an adaptable crew. The lineup scales to fit your event and venue: a tight ensemble for a corporate ballroom or a full battery for a festival stage.

How far in advance should we book?

The earlier the better: popular dates fill quickly, especially in peak event season. If your date is soon, still reach out; the Rhythm Messengers will let you know right away whether the crew is available.
